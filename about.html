Introduction

The Computer Science Department was established to provide quality education in computing and technology. We focus on theoretical foundations and practical skills.

Vision

  • To become a leading department in research and innovation.
  • To produce skilled IT professionals.

Mission

  • Provide quality education.
  • Promote research culture.
  • Encourage innovation and entrepreneurship.

Chairperson Message

"Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, creativity, and strong ethical values to meet global challenges in technology."
